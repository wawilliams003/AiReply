AI Reply

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Reply collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app and keyboard extension.

Effective date: March 28, 2026

1. Overview

AI Reply is designed to help you draft email and message replies using artificial intelligence. Your privacy matters to us. We aim to collect only the information needed to provide and improve the app’s features.

2. Information We May Collect

Text you provide

When you paste or type message content into AI Reply, that text may be processed to generate suggested replies.

App usage information

We may store limited usage information such as generated reply counts, selected tone preferences, and reply history saved by you in the app.

Purchase information

If you subscribe, purchase validation and subscription status may be handled through Apple’s in-app purchase system.

Diagnostics

We may collect limited technical data such as crash logs or performance diagnostics to improve app stability.

3. How We Use Information

  • To generate AI-powered replies based on the text you provide.
  • To save reply history and preferences when those features are enabled in the app.
  • To maintain your subscription status and access to premium features.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.

4. Keyboard Extension

The AI Reply keyboard extension is intended to help generate suggested replies while you type. Content entered into or captured by the keyboard extension may be used only to provide reply suggestions and related app functionality.

We do not sell the text you provide through the keyboard extension.

5. Data Storage

Some data, such as reply history, tone preferences, and app settings, may be stored locally on your device. If cloud-backed or third-party AI services are used to generate replies, the text you submit may be transmitted securely to those service providers for processing.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Reply may rely on third-party services to power certain features, including:

  • AI or language model providers for reply generation
  • Apple for in-app purchases and subscription management
  • Analytics or crash reporting providers, if used

These third parties may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only as needed to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Process subscriptions or purchases
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the rights, safety, and integrity of the app and its users

8. Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as needed to provide the app’s features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Locally stored content may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the saved data.

9. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app or keyboard extension at any time.
  • You can delete saved history or app data from within the app, if supported.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

10. Children’s Privacy

AI Reply is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of AI Reply after changes means you accept the updated policy.
